Privacy Policy for Straight Up on Android

This privacy policy applies to Straight Up version 1.0 and later.

App Permissions

Straight Up requests permission to use your camera and record audio. The app needs this permission in order to access your camera, and detect your posture. In particular, the app uses your camera to take snapshots, process them and determine whether your posture is good or not. All image processing is done locally on the mobile device. The app does not upload any of your images.
